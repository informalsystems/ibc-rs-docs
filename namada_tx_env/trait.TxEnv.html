fn insert_verifier(&mut self, addr: &Address) -> Result<()>

Insert a verifier address. This address must exist on chain, otherwise the transaction will be rejected.

Validity predicates of each verifier addresses inserted in the transaction will validate the transaction and will receive all the changed storage keys and initialized accounts in their inputs.
