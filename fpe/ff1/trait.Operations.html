fn to_be_bytes(&self, radix: u32, b: usize) -> Self::Bytes

Returns a b-byte big-endian representation of the number that is encoded in base radix by this numeral string. The numerals are valued in decreasing order of significance (big-endian order). This corresponds to $STR^{b}{256}(NUM{radix}(X))$ in the NIST spec.
